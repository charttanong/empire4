Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 25/12/2024

At Empire Global Logistics Co., Ltd., we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and improve the functionality of our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

By continuing to browse or use our website, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our use of cookies, you can disable them through your browser settings.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. These files help websites remember your preferences, settings, and actions over time, making your browsing experience more efficient and personalized.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of the website and enable you to navigate and use its features, such as accessing secure areas and submitting forms. These cookies cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about website usage, such as the number of visitors, the pages viewed, and the time spent on the site. This information helps us improve our website’s functionality and content.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver personalized advertisements based on your browsing activity. They help us tailor ads to your interests and ensure that ads you see are relevant to you.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember your preferences and settings, such as language or region, to provide a more personalized experience.

3. Managing Cookies

You have the right to accept or reject cookies when visiting our website. When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner will appear, giving you the option to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete cookies, or set up alerts when a cookie is being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the website and some features may not work as expected.

4. How to Disable Cookies

To manage your cookie settings or disable cookies, you can follow the instructions provided by your browser’s help section. Below are links to instructions for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

5. Third-Party Cookies

Our website may also use third-party cookies for analytics, advertising, and social media purposes. These third parties may track your browsing activity on our website and other websites. Please note that we do not control these third-party cookies, and their use is governed by the respective privacy policies of those third parties.
